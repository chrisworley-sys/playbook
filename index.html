08 / Common Questions

Questions worth answering.

How can you charge $497–$2,500 when agencies charge $15K/year?

Because we built a production system instead of a labor system. Forty years of marketing strategy lives in our process; AI handles the production work that used to take an agency 80 hours. You get the same depth — custom research, persona work, competitor analysis, written content — without paying for the agency's billable hours, account manager, or retainer model. Same plan. Different math.

Will I actually be able to run this myself?

That's what the activation plan exists for. Every week has 2–4 specific tasks, each with hours estimated and "done when" criteria. If you have 4 hours a week, the plan is sized for 4 hours a week. If you've got a marketing assistant or freelancer, it's something you hand to them on day one. The plan assumes you're a smart owner with limited time, not a marketer.

How is this different from hiring a marketing agency?

An agency charges a monthly retainer ($3K–$15K+) to run your marketing for you, ongoing. SuperSymm delivers a one-time custom plan for $497–$2,500 that you own and act on — or hand to whoever you hire next. No retainer, no contract, no monthly bill. If you later decide you'd rather we run it for you, we can — but you're never pushed to.

How long does it take?

Tier 1: 5–7 days. Tier 2: 7–10 days. Tier 3: 10–14 days. The clock starts when we receive your intake form. You'll get progress updates, and the final deliverable comes as a polished, navigable HTML document you can keep, share, and reference.

What do you need from me to build it?

An intake form — about 15–20 minutes to complete. We ask about your business, your customers, your competitors, your current marketing efforts, your tech stack, and your capacity. That, plus our own analysis of your public surfaces, is what we build the playbook from.

What industries do you work with?

Professional and local service businesses — financial advisors, healthcare and dental practices, accounting and law firms, agencies, consultants, and similar. If your business has clients (not just customers) and reputation matters, you're a fit. If you're in a regulated field, we also build plans that respect the advertising rules you have to follow (SEC, FINRA, HIPAA, and others) so nothing we recommend puts you at risk.

What's the difference between the three tiers?

Tier 1 ($497) is the diagnosis — what's working, what's not, who your customers really are, and what to do about it, including how you show up in search and AI. Tier 2 ($1,200) adds an ads plan and a social media plan with specific recommendations. Tier 3 ($2,500) adds a 90-day calendar of what to publish, the actual posts and emails written for you, and follow-up email sequences. Most owners pick Tier 3 because it removes the writing work entirely — you just follow the plan.

What if I'm not happy with the result?

We'll iterate. Every playbook includes one revision round where you can ask for sections to be reworked, deepened, or refocused. If after that the deliverable still doesn't match what you needed, we'll refund the difference. We'd rather have happy customers than locked-in ones.

Can I see what a finished playbook looks like first?

Yes — we built a complete example for a sample business (Meridian Wealth Partners) so you can read every section before paying anything. Open the example →

Can you also run the playbook for me?

Yes — if after reading the playbook you'd rather we execute it, we can. That's a separate engagement and a separate conversation. The playbook itself is built to be runnable by you, your team, or whoever you hire next. Learn more about full-service options →
