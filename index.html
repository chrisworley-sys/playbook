01 / The Idea

What it is — and what it isn't.

A SuperSymm Playbook is a custom marketing plan for your business. Not a template. Not a generic checklist. An actual personalized document, built from real analysis of your business, your industry, your competitors, your customers, and your current digital presence.

It identifies where the opportunities are, which channels to prioritize, what's worth your time, where you're losing customers, and exactly what to do next — all sequenced against the capacity you actually have.

It's the document a small marketing team would build for you over six weeks — except we deliver it in 5 to 14 days for a one-time fee. Yours to keep, share, and execute against.

It is NOT
A retainer, an agency relationship, or a sales call in disguise.
  • No monthly fees
  • No multi-month contracts
  • No discovery calls required
  • No upsell pressure
  • One purchase, one delivery, yours to keep

06 / Common Questions

Questions worth answering.

How is this different from hiring a marketing agency?

An agency charges a monthly retainer ($3K-$15K+) to run your marketing. SuperSymm delivers a one-time custom playbook for $497-$2,500 that you can execute or hand to whoever you hire next. No retainer, no contracts, no monthly burn. If you eventually want us to run it for you, we offer that as a partner engagement — but you're never required to upgrade.

How long does it take?

Tier 1: 5-7 days. Tier 2: 7-10 days. Tier 3: 10-14 days. The clock starts when we receive your intake form. You'll get progress updates, and the final deliverable comes as a polished, navigable HTML document you can keep, share, and reference.

What do you need from me to build it?

An intake form — about 15-20 minutes to complete. We ask about your business, your customers, your competitors, your current marketing efforts, your tech stack, and your capacity. That, plus our own analysis of your public surfaces, is what we build the playbook from.

What industries do you work with?

We work with most service-based businesses, with deep expertise in financial advisors (RIAs), healthcare practices, tax and accounting firms, B2B software companies, and professional services. If you're in a regulated industry, we have compliance-aware playbooks for SEC, FINRA, HIPAA, and other frameworks.

What's the difference between the three tiers?

Tier 1 ($497) is the analytical audit — what's working, what's not, and what to do about it, including SEO and AI search analysis. Tier 2 ($1,200) adds paid media strategy and social media analysis with channel-specific recommendations. Tier 3 ($2,500) adds a 90-day content calendar with ready-to-publish posts, email lifecycle sequences, and a compliance-ready workflow. Most DIY-oriented businesses pick Tier 3 because it eliminates the writing work entirely.

What if I'm not happy with the result?

We'll iterate. Every playbook includes one revision round where you can ask for sections to be reworked, deepened, or refocused. If after that the deliverable still doesn't match what you needed, we'll refund the difference. We'd rather have happy customers than locked-in ones.
